## â“ FAQ

**Q: Are my saved keywords and influencers backed up?**
A: They're stored locally in your browser. Export influencers to CSV for backup. Keyword lists should be copied manually.

**Q: Can I share my keyword lists with my team?**
A: Currently manual - copy/paste the keywords. Future: Export/import feature planned.

**Q: How many keywords/influencers can I save?**
A: No hard limit, but keep it manageable (10-20 lists, 50-100 influencers recommended).

**Q: What happens if I clear my browser data?**
A: Saved data will be lost. Export influencers regularly as backup.

**Q: Can I edit a saved keyword list?**
A: Currently: Delete and recreate. Future: Edit feature planned.

**Q: Do influencers sync across devices?**
A: No, they're stored locally per browser. Export/import to move between devices.
